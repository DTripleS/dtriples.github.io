Lei Wang 王 磊

Ph.D. (Southeast University), Associate Professor, Nanjing Forestry University

Founding Director, Data Science and Systems Science (DTripleS) Lab

Brief CV

Lei Wang received the Ph.D. degree in computer science from Southeast University, China. Prior to this, he received B.S. and M.S. in computer science from Lanzhou University, China. He is an associate professor and founding director of Data Science and Systems Science (DTripleS) Lab, with Nanjing Forestry University, China. He visited the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, during 2019. His research interests mainly include Services Computing, Edge Computing, Software Reliability Engineering, and Financial Big Data Analysis. His publications have appeared in international journals and popular conferences, e.g., the IEEE Transactions on Knowledge and Data Engineering, the IEEE Transactions on Software Engineering, the IEEE Transactions on Parallel and Distributed Systems, the IEEE Transactions on Services Computing, the Journal of Systems and Software, the Journal of Parallel and Distributed Computing, the Knowledge-Based Systems, the IEEE Internet of Things Journal, the Applied Intelligence, ICSOC, ICWS, SCC, etc.
